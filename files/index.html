Latinx Heritage Month

Join us in recognizing Latinx Heritage Month (September 15 – October 15). All SJSU community members are welcome to participate in these events as we come together to honor, learn, and celebrate. This year’s theme, Aquí Estamos: Resilient and Rising, affirms the resilience and enduring contributions of Latinx communities. At San José State, we honor these histories and cultures as part of our broader commitment to creating a future where all Spartans can thrive.

Why Choose SJSU?

San José State provides a world-class education—whether courses are online or in person. The university is expanding the ways we provide high-quality education and unique research and hands-on experiences to students.
